MultiNFT

A Comprehensive Dataset from OpenSea for NFT Price Prediction and Market Analytics

Sourced from OpenSea's official API, this dataset spans four years (May 2021 - April 2025) with comprehensive transaction records across different market cycles. Features 2.38M transactions and 500K standardized assets from top PFP collections, providing authentic data for NFT research and multimodal learning applications.

About

MultiNFT is a comprehensive multimodal dataset of non-fungible tokens (NFTs), sourced from OpenSea (https://opensea.io). To ensure data accuracy and consistency, the dataset is constructed using official APIs and adopts a three-stream data acquisition architecture that integrates sales records, asset metadata, and standardized image processing.


Spanning a four-year period from 1 May 2021 to 30 April 2025, MultiNFT captures 2.38M transactions involving over 500K unique NFT assets. By encompassing multiple market cycles, the dataset offers a rich foundation for research in NFT price prediction, multimodal learning, and digital asset analytics.
